Then $\Lambda=\bigoplus_{i=1}^r\Lambda_i$, ${} \Lambda_i\cong \text{Mat}_{n_i}(D_i)$, ${} D_i$ is a division ring, and the $\Lambda_i$ are uniquely determined.
